Bachelor's Thesis

"Designing Fintech Applications for Older Adults"

This thesis is something I am very proud of. "Designing Fintech Applications for Older Adults" is written together with my friend Hannah Sundberg, and it investigates how Banking apps can be more accessible for older people. This is done by evaluating previous Design Guidleines and Recommendations through Semi-structured Interviews, Usability Testing and Prototyping.

Master's Thesis

"How Do You Want to Watch This?"

My master's thesis is a really fun project if you ask me. And the thing is, it is investigating something almost completely new in the field of study - Data Visualization in Dungeons and Dragons (TTRPG) Livestreaming. In this nerdy context, me and my friend Kajsa Andersson investigated how visual data information overlays affect the viewer engagement in these type of storytelling heavy streams and complex games.
