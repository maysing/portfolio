Internship at Brandperior 2023

The following projects are from my internship at the company Brandperior during the spring of 2023. The company is a marketing agency based in Ulricehamn and I got the opportunity to help with graphic content, marketing, and web design. Scroll down to see the work!

Content for companies

During my internship, I got to work with various companies in the area of Ulricehamn and Borås. I created content for their social media, such as LinkedIn, and also designed web pages in Adobe XD. The work was very varied and I got to be creative with text, videos, and images.

Web design in Adobe XD

During the internship, I got to design various web pages in Adobe XD according to the company's predefined layouts. However, I got to be creative with color and images. One of the websites got published and is for the company Lillsjön.
